Lodging

We have reserved exclusive use of Mosswood Hollow Retreat Center for the weekend from Friday afternoon until Sunday afternoon. It is our hope that all coming from out of town will book their Friday and Saturday night lodging at Mosswood Hollow.

There are a range of options available: private rooms, groups rooms and camping. They are a retreat center so note that many of their rooms contain two single beds and that their prices are per person, not per room. Reservation info can be found on the Mosswood Site.

Get in touch with us if you are arriving earlier than Friday and are looking for a place to stay (mhjkwedding@gmail.com).

Meals

We will be hosting a welcome BBQ on Friday evening and the reception dinner on Saturday evening.

Please send us any dietary restrictions and preferences (mhjkwedding@gmail.com).

Breakfast and lunch on Saturday and Sunday will be available at Mosswood Hollow, but these meals will need to be booked in advance through Mosswood Hollow . The Mosswood Meals come well recommended. Other options are to BYO breakfasts and lunches (no available kitchen) or travel to Duvall (15 mins).

Travel

Mosswood Hollow is about a 15 minute drive from Duvall, WA into the western slope foothills of the cascades.

The closest major airport is the Seattle-Tacoma International Airport. It is about an hour drive from Sea-Tac to Mosswood Hollow. We will likely run an airport shuttle Friday afternoon and Sunday afternoon if there is a need for it. Get in touch with us if that is something you are interested in (mhjkwedding@gmail.com).
